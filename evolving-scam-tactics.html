Evolving Scam Tactics

Staying ahead of the evolving tactics used by scammers is essential. Below are some of the recent scam tactics and related newspaper articles:

Recent Scam Tactics

  • Phishing emails disguised as bank notifications
  • Fake websites that mimic legitimate banking sites
  • Vishing (voice phishing) calls claiming to be from your bank
  • Smishing (SMS phishing) messages with malicious links
  • Social engineering attacks via social media platforms
